Synopsis

Shiyori (a female college student who was cheated on by her boyfriend), persuaded by her best friend Aiko, travels to a remote hot spring village in the country to heal her heartbreak. However, after getting into an argument with Aiko, Shiyori finds herself alone when her mobile phone suddenly rings. A mysterious man warns her: “If you don’t leave the village, I’ll cut off your legs.”
