KASEIFU KUROMI WA KUSATTA KAZOKU WO YURUSANAI (THE HOUSEKEEPER KUROMI WON’T FORGIVE ROTTEN FAMILIES)

Synopsis

A Thrilling Family Horror Drama That Will Shake Your Perception of Family

Kuromi Kiyoka (Nagisa Sekimizu), a housekeeper with secrets of her own, is hired to work for the dysfunctional Haibara family. Her singular mission? To create the “ideal family.” But her methods are far from ordinary—breaking all boundaries of morality and ethics. What drives Kuromi to this dangerous obsession? What shocking truth lies behind her relentless pursuit of perfection?

As the Haibara household teeters between salvation and destruction, Kuromi’s actions raise a chilling question: is she a savior or the catalyst for their doom? With suspenseful twists and a heart-stopping pace, this drama challenges conventional ideas of family and explores the fine line between idealism and reality.
