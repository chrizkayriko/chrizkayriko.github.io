LILAC NO HANASAKU KEMONOMICHI (GOODBYE MY DEAR)

Synopsis

Until three years prior, Kishimoto Satori was a shut-in whose only companion was Pearl, her now deceased dog. Living with her grandmother Chidori, who took her in out of concern, Satori gradually began to recover. Inspired by her love for animals, she decided to become a veterinarian to help save them.

For Satori, who has left her grandmother and taken her first step in an unfamiliar land, everything she sees is new. Studying veterinary medicine for the first time, living with others for the first time, her first part-time job. Her first friendship, her first love…

In the vast land of Hokkaido, she meets university friends, various animals, pet owners, and fellow veterinarians. As she faces numerous trials in the field where life and death hang in the balance, we witness her gradual growth.
